Hello World

I am Jean Remy, the guy you see on your left or probably on up. I'm usually nicknamed "Reyz". 😊
I am a bilingual educator passionate about preparing students not only for academic success, but for the realities of a rapidly evolving, technology-driven world. With over three years of teaching experience across Rwanda and the Democratic Republic of Congo, I bring a dynamic and adaptable approach to both early childhood and primary education. As a modern educator, I believe that teaching must go beyond traditional methods. While I value structure, discipline, and strong academic foundations, I equally emphasize creativity, critical thinking, and student engagement. My classroom is a space where learners are encouraged to ask questions, express themselves confidently, and actively participate in their learning journey. My background in Software Engineering allows me to integrate digital literacy and computational thinking into everyday teaching. I introduce students to logical reasoning, problem-solving, and basic coding concepts at an early stage, helping them develop skills that are essential in the 21st century. I see technology not as a replacement for teaching, but as a powerful tool to enhance understanding and unlock creativity. As a bilingual educator, I am committed to developing strong communication skills in both French and English. I focus on building students’ confidence in expressing ideas clearly, both orally and in writing, preparing them to thrive in diverse and international environments. Beyond academics, I believe in educating the whole child. I strive to create a classroom culture built on respect, responsibility, and integrity. My goal is to guide students to become not only knowledgeable, but also disciplined, curious, and capable individuals who can think independently and contribute meaningfully to society. Ultimately, I see myself not just as a teacher, but as a mentor, a guide, and a lifelong learner—committed to continuous growth and innovation in education..

- Driven by curiosity and purpose, I keep learning to improve and achieve my goals.
